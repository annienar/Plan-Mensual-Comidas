Chocolate Chip Cookies
Makes 24 cookies
Calories: 150 per cookie
Prep time: 20 minutes
Cook time: 12 minutes

Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon (5g) baking soda
1 teaspoon (5g) salt
1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
3/4 cup (165g) packed brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 teaspoon (5ml) vanilla extract
2 cups (340g) semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup (120g) chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
2. In a small bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt
3. In a large bowl, beat butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy
4. Add eggs and vanilla, beat well
5. Gradually beat in flour mixture
6. Stir in chocolate chips and walnuts
7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets
8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown
9. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes
10. Remove to wire racks to cool completely

Notes:
- For chewier cookies, bake for 9 minutes
- For crispier cookies, bake for 11 minutes
- Dough can be refrigerated for up to 3 days
- Cookies can be frozen for up to 3 months
